Community Involvement

Making delicious baked goods is what we do best and we understand that not everybody can afford to pay for our products. It is our responsibility to help people in need.

Sustainability

We value sustainability and one of the ways we practice this is by partnering with small local farmers.

We proudly use Shepherd's Grain Flour for all of our bagels and bread. Along with this, we have committed to using only non-GMO ingredients.
